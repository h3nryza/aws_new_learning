Top 10 Most Impactful Changes (2021-2026)

If you only have time to learn ten things, make it these:

  1. IAM Identity Center replaced AWS SSO July 2022
    Full rebrand with expanded functionality — central identity management across Organizations.
  2. Amazon Bedrock launched — managed GenAI platform Sep 2023
    Access to foundation models (Claude, Llama, Titan) via API with fine-tuning, RAG, and Guardrails.
  3. Amazon Q launched — AI assistant family Apr 2024
    Amazon Q Developer, Q Business, and Q in QuickSight — AI assistants across the AWS ecosystem.
  4. Terraform BSL license change + OpenTofu fork Aug 2023
    HashiCorp switched to Business Source License; community forked OpenTofu under Linux Foundation.
  5. VPC Lattice — new service-to-service networking Mar 2023
    Application-layer networking that works across VPCs, accounts, and compute types (EC2, ECS, EKS, Lambda).
  6. Aurora Serverless v2 replaced v1 Apr 2022
    Instant scaling in fine-grained increments — v1 deprecated. Now the default serverless option.
  7. S3 Express One Zone — millisecond latency storage Nov 2023
    New directory bucket type with single-digit-ms latency, designed for compute-adjacent workloads.
  8. CDK v2 replaced v1 Jun 2022
    Single-package model, all constructs consolidated. CDK v1 entered maintenance then end-of-support.
  9. Organizations: new RCPs and Declarative Policies
    Resource Control Policies join SCPs for governance; Declarative Policies enforce service configurations.
  10. EKS Pod Identity replacing IRSA pattern Nov 2023
    Simpler IAM-to-pod mapping without OIDC provider setup — becoming the recommended approach.

Suggested 1-Month Study Plan

A focused timeline to get back up to speed, assuming you already have SA Pro and DevOps Pro foundational knowledge from 2021.

Week 1: Security & Identity + Exam Blueprints

  • Review IAM Identity Center, Control Tower updates, and Security Hub enhancements
  • Study SCPs, RCPs, and Declarative Policies in Organizations
  • Download and review SAP-C02 and DOP-C02 exam guides — compare to previous versions
  • Identify new domains and shifted weightings

Week 2: DevOps & IaC + Networking

  • Understand CDK v2 changes, Terraform BSL/OpenTofu landscape, CloudFormation updates
  • Review CodePipeline V2, ECS/EKS enhancements, Lambda updates
  • Study VPC Lattice, Cloud WAN, and Network Firewall updates
  • Hands-on: deploy a CDK v2 stack and explore VPC Lattice

Week 3: AI/ML + Compute, Storage & Databases

  • Deep dive into Bedrock, SageMaker updates, and Amazon Q
  • Understand Responsible AI features and Guardrails
  • Review Graviton processors, S3 Express One Zone, Aurora Serverless v2
  • Study DynamoDB and ElastiCache Serverless updates

Week 4: Practice Exams + Weak Area Review

  • Take practice exams for SAP-C02 and DOP-C02
  • Review incorrect answers and identify weak areas
  • Re-study weak areas using the deep-dive section pages
  • Final review of new and renamed services
